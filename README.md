# ServiceNearMe

ServiceNearMe is a Hyper-Local City Services and Business Discovery Platform that helps residents easily find reliable services and local businesses nearby. The platform brings together scattered information about services like electricians, tutors, repair technicians, cafes, restaurants, and other providers into one centralized system.

This project is being developed as part of a Hackathon organized by Arka Jain University.

--------------------------------------------------

## Problem

Residents in a city often struggle to find trustworthy information about nearby services. Information is usually scattered across:

- Social media pages
- Informal recommendations
- Multiple websites or apps

This makes it difficult for people to quickly discover services and also reduces the visibility of many small local businesses.

--------------------------------------------------

## Solution

ServiceNearMe provides a centralized digital platform where users can easily discover and explore nearby services.

The platform allows users to:

- Search for nearby services
- Explore services by category
- View service provider listings
- Read ratings and reviews
- Contact or book services

At the same time, it helps small local businesses increase their visibility and reach more potential customers.

Users interact with the frontend to search or browse services. The backend processes requests and retrieves the relevant data from the database.
